About Me

I've been developing Android apps for a few years now, and I have also been known to juggle. Usually my apps have nothing to do with juggling.

I like development. It's fun. I like public performing, and I like public speaking too. I like helping other developers when I can. I have presented on several occasions at our local Google Developer Group, and I am a co-organiser of the Dublin Android Developer Meetup group.

I have great intentions of writing a technical blog about Android development. So far this includes a couple of articles about a little side project of mine called Watts App. The articles are available here and here.

I have never been a web developer. Or a designer.
